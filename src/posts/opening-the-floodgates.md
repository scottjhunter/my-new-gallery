  <p>
    This blog began as a messy experiment and somehow turned into a working idea.
    It lives at the intersection of artificial intelligence, noetic mysticism, art,
    graphic design, and film editing. I am not trying to make it tidy; I am trying
    to make it true.
  </p>

  <p>
    I used AI to help build this site, and that process was its own education.
    It was not smooth or seamless. The AI got things wrong, I misunderstood
    instructions, and we stumbled our way toward something functional.
  </p>

  <p>
    The plan is to post a mix of content: an image gallery blending AI art with
    real photography, experiments in design, thoughts on the creative process, and
    reflections that do not fit anywhere else.
  </p>
